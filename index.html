About

syber krop coin

Syber Krop Coin (SKC) is a cryptocurrency developed by a team of scholars, Developers and crypto enthusiasts with experience in education and design as well as mining, staking, buying, selling and trading a variety of cryptocurrencies. It's established to push the value of cryptocurrency to everyone. To show the importance of crypto in future and bring them into the world of decentralization. Our teams will be made up of project managers with a specialty in each field, and will be accompanied by a financial expert as well as a legal specialist to validate the compliance and the realization of each project. We guarantee its sustainability and the transparency of the results for the holders of NFTs as well as the community.
Syber krop is built with the main goal of helping new businesses with great ideas find investors. That’s why Syber krop is proud to make a statement about the “Syber Seed” a crowdfunding app to connect investors and innovators. In a large pool of funding applicants each and every funding request will be verified before crowdfunding begins to make sure it’s a win-win deal. It’s completely community driven through voting for the best project the investor thinks it is. The more votes it gets the more attention and investment likely to get. The system is foolproof so there can’t be manipulations or “one-man” decisions. Anyone can apply for funding- there are no rules or criteria for what a project should look like.
KYC for customers:
In order to meet the legal requirements of the different countries, we set up a KYC* to validate the identity of the holders of the NFTs of the different collections, when they wish to recover the dividends and various bonuses inherent to the project. It is possible to hold, resell or purchase NFTs from our collections without validating a KYC*, but it will be necessary to do so in order to receive the bonuses and dividends in future.
